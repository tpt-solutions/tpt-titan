# TPT Titan AI Best Practices Guide

## Maximizing AI Productivity for Small and Medium Businesses

This guide provides proven strategies and best practices for getting the most out of TPT Titan's AI features in your business environment.

## Understanding AI in Business Context

### AI as a Productivity Multiplier

**Not a Replacement**: AI features enhance human capabilities, they don't replace expertise and judgment.

**Best Results**: Combine AI suggestions with your business knowledge for optimal outcomes.

**Continuous Learning**: AI improves with use - provide feedback and corrections to enhance accuracy.

4. **Establish Guidelines**: Create usage policies

#### Phase 2: Integration (Week 3-4)
1. **Workflow Integration**: Incorporate AI into existing processes
2. **Team Training**: Expand training to all users

#### Phase 3: Optimization (Month 2+)
1. **Advanced Features**: Explore complex workflows

## Text Editor AI Best Practices

### Writing Enhancement Strategy

#### Content Types by Effectiveness

**High Impact Areas**:
- Business emails and communications
- Reports and documentation
- Marketing copy and proposals
- Meeting agendas and notes

**Medium Impact Areas**:
- Creative writing and brainstorming
- Technical documentation
- Training materials

**Low Impact Areas**:
- Highly technical or specialized content
- Legal documents requiring precision
- Personal communications

#### Writing Workflow Optimization

1. **Draft First**: Write your initial draft naturally
2. **AI Enhancement**: Use AI for grammar, clarity, and style
3. **Review and Edit**: Apply your expertise to AI suggestions
4. **Final Polish**: Use AI for final proofreading

### Document Summarization

#### When to Use Summaries
- **Meeting Notes**: Convert long discussions to key points
- **Research Reports**: Extract main findings and recommendations
- **Contract Reviews**: Identify key terms and obligations
- **Email Threads**: Get quick overview of conversation history

#### Summary Best Practices
- **Length Matters**: Longer documents (>1000 words) work best
- **Clear Structure**: Well-organized documents summarize better
- **Context Preservation**: AI maintains important context and nuances
- **Review Results**: Always review AI summaries for accuracy

### Voice-to-Text Integration

#### Optimal Usage Scenarios
- **Meeting Transcription**: Real-time note taking
- **Idea Capture**: Quick brainstorming sessions
- **Mobile Productivity**: Hands-free content creation
- **Accessibility**: Alternative input method

#### Voice Input Optimization
- **Clear Environment**: Minimize background noise
- **Natural Speech**: Speak conversationally, not robotically
- **Punctuation Commands**: Use "period", "comma", "new line"
- **Correction Workflow**: Dictate, review, edit as needed

**Priority Categories**:
- Urgent Action Required

#### Productivity Scenarios
- **Quick Responses**: Short acknowledgments and confirmations
- **Standard Communications**: Routine updates and notifications
- **Mobile Productivity**: Composing on-the-go
- **Accessibility**: Alternative to typing

#### Voice Email Best Practices
- **Structure Your Speech**: "Greeting, main message, closing, signature"

- **Length Consideration**: Shorter emails work better with voice

#### Conversion Workflow

2. **AI Conversion**: Let AI extract task details

#### Task Creation Standards
- **Clear Titles**: Descriptive, action-oriented titles

- **Appropriate Deadlines**: Realistic timeframes

## Calendar AI Best Practices

### Voice Event Creation

#### Effective Voice Commands
- **Be Specific**: Include dates, times, locations, and attendees
- **Use Natural Language**: "Schedule team meeting tomorrow at 2 PM in Conference Room A for 90 minutes"
- **Include Context**: Mention purpose and required participants
- **Time Zones**: Specify if different from default

#### Event Creation Workflow
1. **Voice Input**: Dictate event details naturally
2. **AI Processing**: Review and confirm AI interpretation
3. **Add Details**: Include agenda, attachments, or special instructions
4. **Send Invites**: Add attendees and send invitations
